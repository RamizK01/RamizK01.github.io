Covid-19 Stat-Map

The pandemic was a rough time period for many individuals, unfortunately the virus spread globally and many people were infected and even killed. Despite this, the World Health Organization (WHO) and the World Bank Dataset were able to collect useful information regarding case counts and mortality in time sensitive manner. In this project I leveraged R Shiny to create an interactive web app which is able to tell you the recorded case fatality rate, mortality, and prevalence per country.
