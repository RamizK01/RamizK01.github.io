Reimbursement and Procurement for Inspire Medical Devices

Medical Device Reimbursement is a tricky and tedious process...I think the industry can agree that if there was some simple linear path to acheiving this regulatory hurdle then they would much rather prefer it. While it is tricky, the concept of understanding payers money and funding helped me broaden my understanding of the Canadian Healthcare system and the orgranizations involved in this process. Speaking to Key Opinion Leaders and various Stakeholders really elevated my level of understanding for this field of work.

As part of my masters coursework, I (alongside two classmates) proposed a reimbursement plan for a disruptive stage IV medical device looking to solve uncomfortable and potentially fatal in Obstructive Sleep Apnea patients.
